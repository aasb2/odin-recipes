Cake

Description

You can't have your cake and eat it too. Actually you can, you just need to follow this easy recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Prehead the oven to 175 degrees Celsius
  3. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans
  4. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda, and salt all together in a large bowl
  5. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla
  6. Mix for 2 minutes on medium speead with an eletric mixer
  7. Stir in the boiling water (The batter will be thin)
  8. Pour evenly into the prepared pans
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean (about 30 to 35 minutes)
  10. Cool in teh pans for 10 minutes
  11. Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely
  12. Enjoy
